### 2. **Journal (The Void Diary)**
- **Aesthetic**: Pixelated notebook with CRT screen scanlines
- **Features**:
  - Daily entries with timestamps
  - Mood tagging (auto-suggested based on sentiment analysis)

  - **"Echo Reflections"**: AI reads your entries and gives gentle insights weekly
  - Export as a retro "save file" (downloadable .txt with ASCII art borders)

- **Concept**: A digital cork board with a retro twist
- **Features**:
  - Pin images, quotes, goals
  - Pixelated polaroid-style frames
  - Drag-and-drop interface with satisfying *click* sounds
  - Categories: Career, Love, Health, Vibes
  - **"Manifestation Meter"**: Tracks how often you view/update your board

### 4. **Retro Mini-Games (Soul Arcade)**
- **Purpose**: Mindfulness through play
- **Games**:
  - **Pixel Chess**: Classic chess with retro sprites and chiptune music
  - **Breathe Quest**: A rhythm game synced to breathing exercises (inhale/exhale timing)
  - **Mood Tetris**: Tetris but blocks are emotions you're "clearing"
  - **Snake Zen**: Classic Snake but slower, meditative, with calming colors
- **Rewards**: Playing games earns "Soul Coins" to unlock themes/music

### 5. **Echo AI Companion**
- **Personality**: Supportive, slightly sarcastic, retro-game-character vibes
- **Features**:
  - Daily affirmations in pixel speech bubbles
  - Emotional check-ins via chat
  - Suggests journal prompts based on your patterns
  - **"Boss Battle" Mode**: When you're struggling, Echo helps you "defeat" anxiety/stress through guided exercises

### 6. **Retro Theme System**
- **Default**: 90s Game Boy (green monochrome)
- **Unlockables**:
  - Sunset Vaporwave (pink/purple gradients)
  - CRT Nostalgia (scanlines + warm glow)
  - Arcade Neon (bright colors, grid backgrounds)
  - Tamagotchi Pastel (soft colors, rounded pixels)
- **Customization**: Choose pixel fonts, sound effects, color palettes

### Visual Style
- **Pixel Art Everything**: Icons, buttons, illustrations
- **Dithering Effects**: Retro gradient dithering like old monitors
